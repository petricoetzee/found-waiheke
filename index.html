Welcome to Found

Made from Found Things, Full of Found Moments

Step inside and you'll see it straight away — reclaimed timber, riveted copper, salvaged signage and a whole lot of character. Found Waiheke is a relaxed café by day and a buzzing bar by night, built on the simple idea that the best places are the ones that feel a little bit found, not made.
