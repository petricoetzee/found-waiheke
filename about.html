Our Story

About Found

A little bit reclaimed, a little bit retro, and a whole lot of Waiheke.

Built From Found Things

A Space With a Story

Everything about Found has a story — the reclaimed timber, the riveted copper, the hand-painted signage. We built this place from things other people had finished with, and turned it into somewhere new. It's a little bit rustic, a little bit quirky, and it suits Waiheke down to the ground.

Found Waiheke started as a simple idea: a relaxed café lounge for the Surfdale community that could grow into something more — a restaurant, a bar, a place for live music, and a function centre for the island's celebrations.

Meet the Team

The Faces Behind Found

Our team is what makes Found feel like home. Friendly, local and always up for a laugh, they're the reason so many of our regulars keep coming back. Whether you're after a quiet coffee or a big night out, they'll look after you.

We're proud to be part of the Surfdale community and to give visitors and locals alike a place to gather, eat, drink and celebrate.
