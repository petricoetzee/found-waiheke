Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take bookings?+

We welcome walk-ins, but for larger groups or weekend evenings we recommend getting in touch via the form below or messaging us on Facebook to secure a table.

Is there parking nearby?+

Yes — there's parking available on and around Miami Ave, a short walk from the venue.

Can you cater for dietary requirements?+

We're happy to accommodate most dietary requirements where possible — just let our team know when you arrive or mention it in your function enquiry.

Do you host private functions and events?+

Yes — our function centre is available for birthdays, engagements, work functions and more. Send us an enquiry with your event details and we'll help you plan it.

What's the best way to find out about live music nights?+

Follow us on Facebook for the latest live music, DJ nights and event announcements.
